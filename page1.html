Tetromino

Tetris has differently shaped pieces called "Tetrominoes." There are 7 different tetrominoes.

The tetrominoes are the

  • Z Tetromino
  • S Tetromino
  • L Tetromino
  • J Tetromino
  • O Tetromino
  • T Tetromino
  • I Tetromino
